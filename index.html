Oregon Heart of Cascadia
Why Oregon?

The Pacific Northwest of the United States is best known for its beautiful coastline, green interior, rainy weather, and spectacular mountains—and Oregon is at the heart of it all. From hot springs to high deserts, Oregon feels like it's own country with the sheer variety of natural experiences it has to offer. One could spend years of their life exploring the state. Luckily, we've done a bit of that for you to hopefully save some time getting started on your own adventures. That doesn't mean of course that we've covered everything worth covering, or that you won't turn a corner in the wilderness only to find yourself facing a dozen more corners unturned.

Where to start?

Whether you're a dedicated hiker/traveller, local, or just passing through Oregon, you'll need a place to start. We've selected some of the most beautiful and memorable spots that we came across during our own trips and organized them accordingly! Are you looking for something with a certain flavor? Damp green blankets of moss squishing beneath your boots while you lose yourself in a maze of rushing white water and pine, perhaps the feel of the old West and sights of shimmering streams leaking from great stone canyons, or maybe you just want to stare into the cold quiet waters of the Pacific and explore the many monolithic landmarks across the cold gray sands of the coast. Feel free to check out the By Region section.

Heard a lot about the waterfalls Oregon has to offer? Maybe you just want a river hike, or a nice day trip to one of the natural hot springs? Whether or not you know exactly what you're looking for, we're sure you'll find it in the By Type section.

While everyone should make time for nature, maybe your time is valuable. Maybe you have a few hours, maybe you have all day. Maybe you should check out the By Length section.

Still not sure what you're looking for, or you're looking for a full adventure to start with? Check out one of our recommended trips below.

The Umpqua Trip

While there are many forests throughout Oregon, none of them feel quite as mystical and isolated as the Umpqua forest. This trip will take you deep into the heart of the forest and through some of the most stunningly beautiful waterfalls you've ever seen, capped off with Oregon's most magical natural hot springs.
